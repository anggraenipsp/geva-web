About More


SEGMENTING



Our segmentation consists of four sections. First, in the demographic segment, we accommodate a wide range of ages from 18 to 55 years without limiting gender, while focusing on middle to upper class people with better purchasing power levels. Second, in the geographical segment, we are based in Indonesia but also work on international markets such as America, Taiwan, Europe and Japan, to expand influence and introduce products to the global level. Third, the behavioral segment highlights a preference for rattan decorations, where individuals involved in our segmentation have strong interests and tendencies towards furniture, accessories and decorative elements made of rattan. Finally, the psychographic segment involves an individual's psychological predisposition and orientation towards crafts, with a deep interest in handicrafts and an appreciation for creative and unique products.


TARGETING



Our target market includes two types of business relationships, namely Business to Customer (B2C) and Business to Business (B2B). We try to meet the needs and introduce our products to direct consumers and business customers.
